    Plain-language glossary
    Popovics-type curve
    A smooth equation that connects the initial concrete or masonry stiffness to its peak compressive stress.
    Secant modulus
    The slope of a straight line from the origin to a selected point on the stress–strain curve.
    Kinematic hardening
    An idealised metal model in which the yield surface shifts during load reversal, representing a simplified Bauschinger effect.
